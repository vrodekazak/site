10 Best Makeup Products Of 2019 You Need
For The Summer

BY CHLOE LEE
MAY 17, 2019

Believe it or not, some makeup products are meant to look good all year round. Makeup brands are coming up with creative and innovative products that are fit for the summer season! Want to know the secret to looking your best this summer? Here are some beauty products that will definitely bring your A-game.

1. Tarte Icy Betch Eye and Cheek Palette

With this palette, you are definitely guaranteed to look like a mermaid coming out of the water. This product includes beautiful shimmery and blue colors, that are perfectly pigmented. If you’ve ever wanted that mermaid-like smokey eye look, this is the perfect palette for you!

2. Huda Beauty N.Y.M.P.H. All Over Body Highlighter

Yes, you can look like a glowing, bronzed goddess this summer without having to go through the pain of tanning under the sun’s harsh rays! Just put this stuff all over your body and watch heads turn all around.

3. Diorshow On Stage Liquid Eyeliner

I cannot stress enough how important it is to wear waterproof eyeliner during the summer! Whether it be form sweat, oil, or water, regular eyeliner can easily smudge and run down your face. Dior has the perfect eyeliner that is both waterproof, and long-lasting. This product also has a flexible felt tip, making it almost effortless to apply!
